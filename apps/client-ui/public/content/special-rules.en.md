# Special Rules - Combinations (Custom Decks)

These special rules apply only to combinations that are not part of the official game but can occur through custom deck building on this website.

---

## The Dark Eye + any special card

The Dark Eye is a standalone special card and was not designed to interact with other special cards. If it is used together with other special cards, the following rules apply:

- The Dark Eye continues to function fully according to its own rules.
- If the drawn card is a special card, its effects are resolved **as normal**.

**Exception:** The combination with the Werewolf has its own special rule.

---

## The Dark Eye + Werewolf

If The Dark Eye draws and plays a Werewolf, the **normal Werewolf effect does not apply**, because the Werewolf cannot be played directly.

- Instead:
  1. The Werewolf immediately becomes the **new trump setter**.
  2. The player chooses a **new trump suit**.
  3. The previously active trump card is **played by the player** as if it had just been placed.

- This change applies **immediately for the rest of the trick and the entire round**.

---

## Vampire or Witch + Cloud Timing: 25-Year Mode (End of Round)

Combining the newer special cards (Vampire, Witch) with the old Cloud rule (end of round) creates a special case:

### Problem

- In the 30-year version, the Cloud is resolved **immediately after the trick**.
- In the 25-year version, it is resolved **only at the end of the round**.
- The Witch can remove a Cloud that has already been played and reintroduce it later.

This can result in:

- **multiple players winning a trick containing the Cloud**.

### Special Rule

- Only **one Cloud effect is applied**.
- The determining factor is the **last trick in the round that contained the Cloud**.
- Earlier Cloud tricks are ignored.
